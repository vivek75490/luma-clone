Account Executive – Advertising
Location Palo Alto, CA
Employment Type Full time
Location Type Hybrid
Department GTM
Compensation $150K - $250K + Commission

We are seeking a dynamic and results-driven Account Executive to join our growing sales team and focus on advertising partnerships. You will be responsible for driving revenue growth by selling our AI-powered creative solutions to advertising agencies, brands, and media companies. You will develop and execute strategic sales plans to expand our presence in the advertising industry.

You thrive in a fast-paced startup environment and have a proven track record of exceeding sales targets in the advertising or technology space. You understand the unique challenges and opportunities in the advertising industry and can effectively communicate the value of AI-powered creative tools to potential clients.

Responsibilities

  • Develop and execute strategic sales plans to achieve revenue targets in the advertising sector
  • Build and maintain relationships with key decision-makers at advertising agencies and brands
  • Conduct product demonstrations and presentations to showcase our AI capabilities
  • Negotiate complex deals and contracts with enterprise clients
  • Collaborate with marketing and product teams to develop targeted campaigns
  • Stay current with industry trends and competitive landscape
  • Manage the full sales cycle from prospecting to closing
  • Provide market feedback to product and marketing teams
  • Attend industry events and conferences to build relationships
  • Mentor and support junior sales team members

Background

  • 5+ years of experience in B2B sales, with a focus on advertising, media, or technology
  • Proven track record of exceeding sales targets and managing complex enterprise deals
  • Strong understanding of the advertising industry and creative workflows
  • Experience selling to advertising agencies, brands, or media companies
  • Excellent communication and presentation skills
  • Ability to understand and articulate technical concepts to non-technical audiences
  • Strong negotiation and contract management skills
  • Experience with CRM systems (Salesforce preferred)
  • Ability to work in a fast-paced, high-growth environment
  • Bachelor's degree in Business, Marketing, or related field preferred

What We Offer

  • Competitive base salary and uncapped commission structure
  • Comprehensive health, dental, and vision benefits
  • Flexible work arrangements and remote-friendly culture
  • Professional development opportunities
  • Collaborative and innovative work environment
  • Opportunity to shape the future of AI in advertising

Note: This role requires experience in advertising sales or related field
