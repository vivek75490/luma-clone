Background

  • Strong programming skills in Python, C++, and CUDA
  • Deep understanding of distributed training and parallel computing

  • Experience with high-performance computing and cluster management
  • Understanding of numerical optimization and linear algebra
  • Experience with profiling and performance analysis tools
  • Master's degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or related field (PhD preferred)
