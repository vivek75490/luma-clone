Sr Manager, People Operations
Location Palo Alto, CA
Employment Type Full time
Location Type Hybrid
Department People & Talent
Compensation $180K - $250K

We are seeking a strategic and experienced Senior Manager of People Operations to join our growing team and help build the foundation for our people programs and processes. You will be responsible for developing and implementing comprehensive people operations strategies that support our rapid growth and ensure an exceptional employee experience. You will partner closely with leadership to create scalable processes and programs that attract, retain, and develop top talent.

You thrive in a fast-paced startup environment and have a proven track record of building and scaling people operations functions. You understand the unique challenges of growing teams in emerging technology markets and can effectively balance strategic thinking with hands-on execution.

Responsibilities

  • Develop and implement comprehensive people operations strategies and programs
  • Own end-to-end employee lifecycle processes including onboarding, offboarding, and performance management
  • Design and implement scalable HR policies and procedures that align with company culture and values
  • Partner with leadership to develop and execute people programs that support business objectives
  • Manage HRIS implementation and optimization to streamline people processes
  • Lead employee engagement initiatives and culture-building programs
  • Develop and implement compensation and benefits strategies
  • Ensure compliance with employment laws and regulations
  • Build and manage relationships with external vendors and partners
  • Analyze people metrics and provide insights to leadership for data-driven decision making

Background

  • 8+ years of experience in people operations, HR, or related field in high-growth technology companies
  • Proven track record of building and scaling people operations functions from the ground up
  • Experience with HRIS implementation and management (Workday, BambooHR, or similar)
  • Strong understanding of employment laws and regulations
  • Excellent communication and interpersonal skills with the ability to build relationships at all levels
  • Data-driven approach with experience using people analytics to inform decisions
  • Ability to work in a fast-paced, high-growth environment
  • Experience with compensation and benefits program design
  • Strong project management skills with the ability to manage multiple priorities
  • Bachelor's degree in Human Resources, Business, or related field preferred
  • PHR, SPHR, or SHRM certification preferred

What We Offer

  • Competitive salary and equity package
  • Comprehensive health, dental, and vision benefits
  • Flexible work arrangements and remote-friendly culture
  • Professional development opportunities
  • Collaborative and innovative work environment
  • Opportunity to shape the future of people operations at AIGETAI

Note: This role requires experience in people operations within the technology sector
