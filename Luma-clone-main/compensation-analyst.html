Compensation Analyst
Location Palo Alto, CA
Employment Type Full time
Location Type Hybrid
Department Business Operations
Compensation Estimated Base Salary $90K - $145K • Offers Equity

You'll shape the way we reward, motivate, and retain the talented minds building our multimodal AI future. You'll own our compensation strategy, bringing clarity, fairness, and innovation to a critical part of Luma’s growth journey, working directly alongside leadership, HR, and finance teams.

What a Typical Week Might Look Like

  • Crunching numbers and turning compensation data into actionable insights—your analytics directly influencing hiring and retention decisions.
  • Collaborating with HR and recruiting teams, guiding them through salary offers and job leveling conversations.
  • Conducting detailed market analysis using tools like Mercer and Radford to ensure our compensation remains competitive and equitable.
  • Developing models and scenarios to help leadership visualize compensation strategies aligned with Luma’s ambitious growth plans.
  • Updating job architectures and compensation structures as we scale, ensuring fairness and transparency remain core to our values.
  • Keeping a pulse on market trends and proactively recommending adjustments to stay ahead in a rapidly evolving AI landscape.

Your Background Looks Something Like

The Essentials:
  • 3-5 years in compensation, finance, or analytical roles, ideally in fast-paced tech or startup environments.
  • Expert-level spreadsheet skills—you love creating pivot tables, complex models, and scenario analyses.
  • Hands-on experience with salary benchmarking surveys like Mercer, Radford, and Pave.
  • Strong grasp of equity compensation, private company valuation, and option pricing (e.g., 409A).
What Would Make You Exceptional:
  • Experience using AI tools to automate workflows or enhance compensation analytics.
  • A knack for translating intricate data insights into clear, compelling narratives for leadership.
  • Proven comfort navigating ambiguity and fluidly shifting priorities in a startup context.

You Might Really Thrive Here If…

  • You’re energized by solving complex puzzles—especially ones involving data and human psychology.
  • You genuinely care about fairness and transparency, and you’re excited to build systems that reflect those values.
  • You can seamlessly move between deep spreadsheet work and lively discussions about team growth.
  • You appreciate the fast-paced unpredictability of startups and have the adaptability to match it.
  • You’re intrigued by how compensation shapes company culture and motivates teams.

Some of the Cool Things We're Working On

  • Scaling our compensation infrastructure from scratch to support our explosive growth.
  • Exploring creative approaches to equity and rewards that resonate with a diverse and innovative team.
  • Leveraging AI-driven tools for more dynamic, predictive compensation insights.
  • Designing strategies to support and attract global talent in competitive AI markets.
  • Ensuring our compensation philosophy actively supports and enhances our inclusive company culture.
